Admission Process

To apply for admission, please follow these steps:

  1. Visit the official website of the institute.
  2. Fill out the online application form.
  3. Submit required documents including educational certificates and ID proof.
  4. Pay the admission fee through the specified payment method.
  5. Attend the Matric Result Card .
  6. Wait for the admission confirmation notification.
  7. Starting From 6 Months To 2 Year
  8. Complete the enrollment process as per the instructions provided.

Elagibility Students

  1. Students must have completed their Matriculation (10th grade) or equivalent from a recognized board.
  2. A minimum percentage of marks may be required, depending on the course applied for.
  3. Some courses may have specific subject requirements (e.g., Science subjects for technical courses).
  4. Age limit criteria may apply for certain programs.
  5. Students should meet any additional criteria specified by the institute for particular courses.
