Mission

Our mission is to enhance global competitiveness in Punjab by developing a quality and productive workforce through demand-driven, standardized, dynamic, and integrated technical education and vocational training services.

History

Govt. Technical Training Institute, Gulberg Lahore, was established in 1980 with the aim of providing quality technical education and vocational training to the youth of Lahore. Over the years, it has evolved into a premier institution under the Punjab Technical Education and Vocational Training Authority (TEVTA),

Vision

o be a leading institution in technical education and vocational training, recognized for excellence in teaching, research, and community service, contributing to the socio-economic development of the region.

Courses Offered

We offer a variety of courses in fields such as Computer Hardware, Welding, Industrial Electronics, Electronic Erection, and Electrician training. Our programs are designed to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge to excel in their respective industries.

Facilities

Our institute is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, including modern classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, and workshops. We also provide access to online resources and libraries to support our students' learning experiences.
